  • The study of facts, events, individuals and society in the past is known as History. Right from the time man realized what it is like to walk on land to creating aeroplanes, history is capturing incidents, inventions, discoveries which developed the society we live in.
  • This course is suitable for all level of students (School to graduate level) & working professionals who want to gain their knowledge of Historical Science and wants to know more about what occurred in the world and how it affected us.
  • Ancient History: The branch covers to the past events dating back to the beginning of writing and recording of events, incidents, discoveries and innovations. The study of Ancient History extends as far as post-classical history. This branch covers all continents inhabited by humans in the period of 3000 BC to AD 500. This involves the study of Harappa-Mohenjadaro Civilazation, Chola dynasty, Western History and much more.
  • Medieval History: This branch covers historic events which happened between the Ancient Period and Modern Period, which expands from the 6th to the 14th centuries. People learn about the Mughal Empire in India, the history of China to the Gupta Empires.
  • Modern History: This branch deals with history which has been coming down from the 15th century onwards, especially the British colonized India. This covers the empires, rules to the world wars to democracy to the industrial revolution. There are World History and Indian History under this branch.
  • The course ends with a detailed analysis of all the major consequences of these historical events and how it affected the world in the long run.
